BM Online

Banque Misr Digital Banking

What is BM Online?

BM Online is Banque Misr's comprehensive digital banking platform that brings your entire bank into your smartphone. As one of Egypt's oldest and largest banks, Banque Misr serves millions of customers, and BM Online gives them 24/7 access to manage accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, buy saving certificates, apply for loans, and much more — all without visiting a branch.

Imagine never having to wait in line at the bank again. With BM Online, you can check your account balance at midnight, transfer money to a friend on Sunday, pay your phone bill at 3 AM, or even apply for a personal loan from your couch. The app puts full banking capabilities in your pocket, available anytime, anywhere.

BM Online is especially powerful for managing saving certificates — a popular investment product in Egypt. You can browse available certificates, compare interest rates, and purchase them instantly through the app, earning competitive returns on your savings without any paperwork or branch visits.

Key Features

Complete Account Management

View balances, transaction history, and account statements for all your Banque Misr accounts in one place.

Instant Money Transfers

Transfer money between your accounts, to other Banque Misr customers, or to any Egyptian bank via InstaPay.

Buy Saving Certificates

Purchase Banque Misr's high-interest saving certificates directly from the app — no branch visit required.

Bill Payments & Services

Pay bills through Fawry integration, top up mobile credit, and manage standing orders for recurring payments.

How It Works

BM Online securely connects to your Banque Misr account using multi-layer security including your username, password, and one-time passwords (OTP) sent to your registered mobile number. Every transaction you perform through the app is encrypted and protected. When you want to check your balance, the app fetches real-time data from Banque Misr's servers. When you transfer money, it's processed through the bank's secure payment infrastructure with instant confirmation. The app works just like visiting a bank branch — except it's faster, more convenient, and available 24/7.

Getting Started

1

Open a Banque Misr account (if you don't have one)

Visit any Banque Misr branch and bring the following:

  • • Original National ID
  • • Proof of address (utility bill or rental contract)
  • • Minimum opening deposit (usually EGP 500–1,000)

A bank representative will help you choose the right account type — savings, current, or salary account.

💡 Account opening typically takes 30–60 minutes.

2

Download and install BM Online

Search "BM Online" or "Banque Misr" in the App Store or Google Play. Look for the Banque Misr logo (red and white). You can also access it on your computer via the website.

💡 The mobile app offers a better experience than the web version.

3

Register for BM Online service

Tap "Register" and provide the following information:

  • • Banque Misr account number
  • • National ID
  • • Registered mobile number
  • • Debit card number and PIN

The app will send an OTP to your registered mobile number to complete verification.

💡 If you don't remember your registered number, call 19888.

4

Create your login credentials

Create a unique username, a strong password, and set up security questions. Write down your username and password somewhere safe for future reference.

💡 Enable biometric login (fingerprint or Face ID) after your first login for quicker, more secure access.

5

Explore the app features

The dashboard shows your current balance, recent transactions, and quick action buttons. Available features include:

  • • Transfers
  • • Bill Payments
  • • Saving Certificates
  • • Cards
  • • Loans
  • • Statements

💡 Take time to explore all menus to discover every available service.

6

Make your first transaction

To make a transfer, follow these steps:

  • • Tap "Transfer"
  • • Choose type (Own Account / Other Banque Misr / InstaPay)
  • • Enter recipient details
  • • Enter amount
  • • Review and confirm with OTP

Transactions receive instant confirmation and are reflected in your balance immediately.

💡 Save frequent recipients to your "Beneficiary List" for faster future transfers.

Pro Tips

• Use the app to buy Banque Misr saving certificates with competitive interest rates — no branch visit needed.
• Set up transaction alerts for real-time SMS or push notifications on every account activity.
• Apply for personal loans, credit cards, and other services directly through BM Online.
• Export account statements for tax and accounting purposes with just a few taps.
• Use the "Quick Balance" feature to check your balance without going through a full login.

Security Warning

Never share your BM Online username, password, or OTP codes with anyone — not even someone claiming to be from Banque Misr. The bank will never ask for this information via phone, email, or SMS.

If you receive suspicious messages or calls asking for your banking details, hang up immediately and call Banque Misr's official customer service at 19888 to report it. Always verify you're using the official BM Online app downloaded from legitimate app stores.
