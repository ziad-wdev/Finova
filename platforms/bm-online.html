BM Online

Banque Misr Digital Banking

What is BM Online?

BM Online is Banque Misr's comprehensive digital banking platform that brings your entire bank into your smartphone. As one of Egypt's oldest and largest banks, Banque Misr serves millions of customers, and BM Online gives them 24/7 access to manage accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, buy saving certificates, apply for loans, and much more - all without visiting a branch.

Imagine never having to wait in line at the bank again. With BM Online, you can check your account balance at midnight, transfer money to a friend on Sunday, pay your phone bill at 3 AM, or even apply for a personal loan from your couch. The app puts full banking capabilities in your pocket, available anytime, anywhere.

BM Online is especially powerful for managing saving certificates - a popular investment product in Egypt. You can browse available certificates, compare interest rates, and purchase them instantly through the app, earning competitive returns on your savings without any paperwork or branch visits. It's modern banking made simple.

Main Features

Complete Account Management

View balances, transaction history, and account statements for all your Banque Misr accounts in one place.

Instant Money Transfers

Transfer money between your accounts, to other Banque Misr customers, or to any Egyptian bank via InstaPay.

Buy Saving Certificates

Purchase Banque Misr's high-interest saving certificates directly from the app - no branch visit required.

Bill Payments & Services

Pay bills through Fawry integration, top up mobile credit, and manage standing orders for recurring payments.

How It Works

BM Online securely connects to your Banque Misr account using multi-layer security including your username, password, and one-time passwords (OTP) sent to your registered mobile number. Every transaction you perform through the app is encrypted and protected. When you want to check your balance, the app fetches real-time data from Banque Misr's servers. When you transfer money, it's processed through the bank's secure payment infrastructure with instant confirmation. The app works just like visiting a bank branch - except it's faster, more convenient, and available 24/7. All your transactions are logged and can be reviewed anytime in your transaction history.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1

Open a Banque Misr account (if you don't have one)

To use BM Online, you need an active bank account at Banque Misr. If you don't have one, visit any Banque Misr branch with:

  • • Your original National ID card
  • • Proof of address (utility bill or rental contract)
  • • Minimum opening deposit (varies by account type, usually EGP 500–1,000)

The bank representative will help you choose the right account type (savings, current, or salary account) and complete the paperwork.

💡 Account opening typically takes 30–60 minutes. You'll receive your debit card and account details immediately or within a few days.

2

Download and install BM Online

Search for "BM Online" or "Banque Misr" in the Apple App Store (for iPhone) or Google Play Store (for Android). Download the official Banque Misr app.

Make sure you download the correct app - look for the Banque Misr logo (red and white colors) and verify the developer is "Banque Misr."

💡 You can also access BM Online from your computer by visiting Banque Misr's website, but the mobile app offers a better experience.

3

Register for BM Online service

Open the app and tap "Register" or "Sign Up." You'll need to provide:

  • • Your Banque Misr account number (found on your debit card or account statement)
  • • National ID number
  • • Registered mobile phone number (the number you gave the bank when opening your account)
  • • Debit card number and PIN

The app will send an OTP (one-time password) to your registered mobile number. Enter this code to verify your identity.

💡 If you don't remember your registered mobile number, visit a branch or call Banque Misr customer service at 19888 to update it.

4

Create your login credentials

After verification, you'll create:

  • • A unique username (must be unique across all BM Online users)
  • • A strong password (mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
  • • Security questions and answers for account recovery

Write down your username and password in a safe place. You'll need them to log in every time.

💡 For faster access, enable biometric login (fingerprint or Face ID) after your first login. This is more secure and convenient.

5

Explore the app features

Once logged in, you'll see your account dashboard showing:

  • • Current balance for all your accounts
  • • Recent transactions
  • • Quick action buttons (Transfer, Pay Bills, etc.)

Common features you can use:

  • • Transfers: Send money to other accounts or banks
  • • Bill Payments: Pay utilities and services via Fawry
  • • Saving Certificates: Browse and purchase investment certificates
  • • Cards: Manage your debit/credit cards, block lost cards
  • • Loans: Apply for personal, car, or housing loans
  • • Statements: Download account statements in PDF

💡 Take a few minutes to explore all the menus and features. BM Online offers almost everything you'd do at a branch.

6

Make your first transaction

Let's try a simple transfer between your own accounts (if you have multiple) or to a friend:

  • 1. Tap "Transfer" or "Send Money"
  • 2. Choose transfer type (Own Account, Other Banque Misr Customer, InstaPay to other banks)
  • 3. Enter the recipient details (account number or mobile number for InstaPay)
  • 4. Enter the amount and optional note
  • 5. Review the details carefully
  • 6. Confirm with OTP sent to your mobile

You'll receive instant confirmation when the transfer is complete. The recipient gets the money immediately (for InstaPay and internal transfers).

💡 Save frequent recipients to your "Beneficiary List" for faster future transfers.

Pro Tips

• Use the app to buy Banque Misr saving certificates with competitive interest rates
• Set up transaction alerts to monitor all account activity in real time via SMS or push notifications
• You can apply for personal loans, credit cards, and other services directly through BM Online
• Export account statements for tax and accounting purposes from the app
• Use the "Quick Balance" feature to check your balance without logging in fully

Important Security Warning

Never share your BM Online username, password, or OTP codes with anyone - not even someone claiming to be from Banque Misr. The bank will never ask for this information via phone, email, or SMS.

If you receive suspicious messages or calls asking for your banking details, hang up immediately and call Banque Misr's official customer service at 19888 to report it. Always verify you're using the official BM Online app downloaded from legitimate app stores.
