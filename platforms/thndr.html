Thndr

Commission-Free Stock Investing

What is Thndr?

Thndr is Egypt's first commission-free investing app that lets you buy and sell stocks on the Egyptian Exchange (EGX) with zero trading fees. It's designed for both beginners and experienced investors, offering a simple smartphone interface with real-time market data, educational content, and instant trade execution.

Traditionally, investing in the stock market required opening a brokerage account with high minimum deposits, paying hefty commissions on every trade, and dealing with complicated procedures. Thndr changes all that. You can start investing with as little as EGP 1, pay zero commission on buying or selling stocks, and manage everything from your phone in just a few taps.

Whether you want to invest in major Egyptian companies like Commercial International Bank (CIB), Talaat Moustafa Group (TMG), or Eastern Tobacco, Thndr makes it accessible to everyone. The app provides stock information, price charts, company news, and even curated collections of stocks to help beginners understand where to start. It's regulated by Egypt's Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA).

Features

Zero Commissions

Buy and sell Egyptian stocks with no trading fees. Keep 100% of your returns - Thndr doesn't charge commissions.

Start with EGP 1

No minimum investment required. Begin your investing journey with any amount you're comfortable with.

Real-Time Trading

Live stock prices and instant order execution during Egyptian Exchange market hours (Sunday–Thursday, 10 AM – 2:30 PM).

Learn as You Invest

Built-in educational content, stock insights, and beginner-friendly guides help you make informed decisions.

How It Works

Thndr connects you directly to the Egyptian Exchange (EGX) through a licensed brokerage firm (EFG Hermes). When you place an order to buy a stock, Thndr sends it to the exchange instantly, and if a seller is available at that price, the trade happens immediately. The stocks you buy are held in your name in a secure custody account regulated by Egypt's financial authorities. You can sell your stocks anytime during market hours, and the funds are transferred to your Thndr wallet, from which you can withdraw to your bank account.

Getting Started

1

Download the Thndr app

Available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Search for "Thndr" and look for the purple logo with a lightning bolt.

💡 Thndr is mobile-only. There's no desktop version.

2

Sign up and verify your identity

Provide the following information to create your account:

  • • Full name (as on National ID)
  • • Mobile number and email address
  • • National ID number

Take a selfie and upload the front and back of your National ID for KYC verification. AI-powered verification typically takes just 1–2 minutes.

💡 Make sure your ID is valid and not expired.

3

Add money to your Thndr wallet

You can fund your wallet through multiple methods:

  • • Bank transfer (from any Egyptian bank account)
  • • Mobile wallet (Vodafone Cash, Fawry)
  • • Credit/Debit card (instant)

Minimum deposit is EGP 1, but most methods require EGP 50–100. Card and wallet deposits are instant.

💡 Start small if you're a beginner.

4

Explore stocks and do your research

Browse all stocks listed on the EGX with access to:

  • • Real-time prices and historical charts
  • • Company profiles and news
  • • Curated collections - Top Gainers, Most Active, Blue Chips

💡 Don't invest in something you don't understand.

5

Place your first trade

Tap a stock → Tap "Buy" → Enter the number of shares or the amount → Review your order → Confirm. Your order is sent to the exchange immediately.

Execute trades during EGX market hours (Sunday–Thursday, 10 AM – 2:30 PM). Purchased stocks appear in your "Portfolio" tab.

💡 Start with a small amount to get familiar with the process.

6

Monitor and sell when ready

Track all your holdings in the Portfolio tab, including:

  • • Current market value
  • • Total profit/loss
  • • Daily price changes

To sell: tap a stock → "Sell" → enter number of shares → confirm. Funds are added to your wallet instantly. You can withdraw to your bank account anytime.

💡 Avoid panicking when prices drop temporarily.

Pro Tips

• Start small and diversify across multiple stocks to reduce risk
• Trading only available during EGX market hours (Sunday–Thursday, 10 AM – 2:30 PM)
• Use Thndr's educational content to learn before you start investing
• Monitor your portfolio regularly but avoid making emotional decisions
• Thndr offers fractional shares for some stocks

Investment Risk Warning

Investing in stocks carries risk. Stock prices can go up or down, and you may lose some or all of your invested capital. Never invest money you can't afford to lose or money you need for essential expenses.

Always do your own research before buying any stock. Understand the company, its business model, and financial health. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
