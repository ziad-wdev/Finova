Fawry

Egypt's #1 Bill Payment & e-Commerce Platform

What is Fawry?

Fawry is Egypt's largest and most trusted electronic payment platform, processing over 4 million transactions every single day. Founded in 2008, Fawry has fundamentally transformed how Egyptians pay for services - replacing long queues and office visits with fast, convenient digital payments available around the clock.

With over 300,000 service points spread across pharmacies, supermarkets, Fawry kiosks, ATMs, and online platforms, paying your bills has never been more accessible. Whether you're in central Cairo, Alexandria, or a small town across Egypt, a Fawry payment point is never far from your doorstep.

Fawry works seamlessly with virtually every major service provider in Egypt - from electricity and water utilities to telecom companies, government agencies, schools, universities, and insurance providers. It is the true one-stop solution for all your everyday payment needs.

300,000+ Payment Points

How It Works

Fawry acts as a trusted bridge between you and service providers across Egypt. When you want to pay a bill, simply select the service in the myFawry app or visit any Fawry point. The system generates a unique reference code tied to your account. You can then pay using your digital wallet, a credit or debit card, or by handing cash to any Fawry agent. Once payment is confirmed, Fawry instantly forwards it to the service provider and issues you a digital receipt - the entire process takes just seconds, with no need to visit the provider's offices in person.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1

Create a myFawry account

Download the myFawry app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit myfawry.com. Sign up using your mobile number and National ID. Verify your account with the SMS code you receive, then set a secure password.

💡 A digital wallet is automatically created for your account upon registration.

2

Browse available services

Once logged in, explore the full range of available payment categories. Common categories include:

  • Utilities - EDCO, EEDCO, BEDCO, and other electricity providers
  • Telecom - mobile top-ups and internet subscriptions
  • Government - traffic fines, passport fees, and official documents
  • Education - school and university fees
  • Insurance - premiums for major Egyptian insurance companies

💡 Use the search bar at the top of the app to quickly find the service you need.

3

Enter your account details and generate a code

Select your service from the list, then enter your meter number, subscriber number, or account number as required. The app will automatically fetch your current bill amount. Once confirmed, tap "Generate Fawry Code" to create your unique payment reference.

💡 Double-check your account or meter number before generating the code to avoid paying the wrong account.

4

Choose how to pay

Fawry offers multiple flexible payment methods to suit everyone:

  • In-app - pay directly using your myFawry wallet or a saved credit/debit card
  • Cash - take your Fawry reference code to any Fawry agent or kiosk and pay in cash
  • Bank app - many Egyptian banks allow Fawry payments directly within their apps

💡 In-app card payments are processed instantly. Cash payments at agents may take a few minutes to reflect.

5

Receive your receipt

The moment your payment is processed, you will receive an SMS confirmation on your registered mobile number. Your full digital receipt is also saved automatically in the app under "Transaction History" for future reference.

💡 You can email or print receipts directly from the app - handy when proof of payment is required by a utility company or government office.

Pro Tips

• Save recurring bills in the myFawry app for one-tap payments - no more re-entering meter numbers every month.
• Fawry charges a small convenience fee - usually EGP 1–5 per transaction depending on the service type.
• You can pay school and university fees for many major Egyptian educational institutions directly through Fawry.
• Fawry Plus cards are available at pharmacies and kiosks nationwide and can be used to top up your myFawry wallet with cash.

Important Warning

Double-check your meter number or subscriber number before confirming any payment. Payments made to the wrong account may be difficult or impossible to reverse, and the process of recovering funds can be time-consuming.
