Vodafone Cash

Egypt's Leading Mobile Wallet

What is Vodafone Cash?

Vodafone Cash is one of Egypt's most widely adopted mobile wallets, operated by Vodafone Egypt. It allows you to store money digitally, pay bills, transfer funds, receive salaries, and shop online - all from a basic mobile phone, no smartphone required.

Imagine carrying a virtual wallet in your pocket that works everywhere. With Vodafone Cash, you can pay your electricity, water, and internet bills without standing in queues. You can shop at thousands of stores, send money to friends and family instantly, and even receive your salary directly into your wallet. All you need is your Vodafone mobile number.

The service works on any phone - even basic feature phones using the simple *9# menu. Over 100,000 Vodafone agents across Egypt can help you deposit or withdraw cash from your wallet whenever you need. It's fast, convenient, and secure.

Main Features

Works on Any Phone

No smartphone needed! Use the *9# USSD menu on any mobile phone to access your wallet and make transactions.

Pay Bills Easily

Pay electricity, water, gas, internet, mobile top-ups, and government bills in seconds from your wallet.

100,000+ Agent Points

Deposit or withdraw cash at Vodafone stores, pharmacies, supermarkets, and kiosks nationwide.

Receive Salary & Remittances

Employers can pay your salary directly to your wallet. Also accepts international remittances from Western Union.

How It Works

Your Vodafone Cash wallet is tied to your Vodafone SIM card and backed by a partner bank (Banque Misr). When you add money to your wallet, it's stored securely in your digital account. You can then use this balance to pay merchants, send money to other Vodafone Cash users instantly, or cash out at any authorized agent. The wallet is protected by a secure PIN that only you know, and every transaction sends you an instant SMS confirmation.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1

Get a Vodafone SIM card

You need an active Vodafone Egypt mobile number to use Vodafone Cash. If you're already a Vodafone subscriber, you're all set! If not, visit any Vodafone store with your National ID to get a new SIM card.

💡 Non-Vodafone users can also register at Vodafone stores and link the service to their existing number from other networks.

2

Register your wallet

From your Vodafone number, dial *9# and follow the on-screen prompts to create your wallet. You'll need to set a secure 4-digit PIN code. Remember this PIN - you'll use it for every transaction.

For full activation (higher limits), visit a Vodafone store with your National ID for biometric verification (fingerprint scan). This takes only a few minutes.

💡 Basic registration gives you up to EGP 5,000 limit. Full KYC (Know Your Customer) verification increases this to EGP 30,000.

3

Add money to your wallet

There are several ways to top up your Vodafone Cash wallet:

  • • Visit any Vodafone agent (over 100,000 locations) and give them cash
  • • Deposit cash at Vodafone Cash-enabled ATMs
  • • Transfer from your bank account via online banking or InstaPay
  • • Receive salary deposits from your employer

💡 Top-ups are instant. You'll receive an SMS confirming the new balance immediately.

4

Pay bills and services

Dial *9# from your Vodafone number, select "Pay Bills," and choose the service you want to pay (electricity, water, internet, mobile bills, etc.). Enter your account or meter number, confirm the amount, and enter your PIN.

For a better experience, download the free Vodafone Cash app from the App Store or Google Play. It has a visual interface that makes bill payment even easier.

💡 Save your recurring bills in the app for one-tap payments next time.

5

Send money to others

To send money to another Vodafone Cash user, dial *9#, select "Send Money," enter their Vodafone mobile number and the amount, then confirm with your PIN. The transfer is instant and free.

You can also send money to bank accounts using the InstaPay integration built into Vodafone Cash.

💡 Transfers between Vodafone Cash users are usually free. Check the app for current fees on bank transfers.

Pro Tips

• Wallet limit: up to EGP 30,000 for verified accounts
• Cash out at any Vodafone agent or ATM displaying the Vodafone Cash logo
• Use the app for a better experience than the USSD (*9#) menu
• Vodafone Cash can receive international remittances from services like Western Union

Important Warning

Keep your PIN confidential. Vodafone Cash agents will never ask for your PIN. Report any suspicious activity immediately by calling 888.
