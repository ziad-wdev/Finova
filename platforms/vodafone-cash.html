Vodafone Cash

Egypt's Leading Mobile Wallet

What is Vodafone Cash?

Vodafone Cash is Egypt's most widely used mobile wallet service, operated by Vodafone Egypt in partnership with Banque Misr. It allows you to store money digitally on your mobile phone and perform a wide range of financial transactions — without needing a traditional bank account.

Think of Vodafone Cash as a virtual pocket linked to your Vodafone SIM card. You can deposit cash, pay bills, send money to friends and family, receive your salary, and even shop online — all from your phone. Your wallet balance is fully insured and protected by Egyptian banking regulations, giving you peace of mind that your money is safe.

Unlike many digital payment solutions that require a smartphone, Vodafone Cash works on any mobile phone. Through the simple *9# USSD menu, even basic feature phones can access the full range of wallet services — making it truly accessible to everyone across Egypt, regardless of device type or internet connectivity.

Main Features

Works on Any Phone

No smartphone needed! Use the *9# USSD menu on any mobile phone to access your wallet and make transactions.

Pay Bills Easily

Pay electricity, water, gas, internet, mobile top-ups, and government bills in seconds from your wallet.

100,000+ Agent Points

Deposit or withdraw cash at Vodafone stores, pharmacies, supermarkets, and kiosks nationwide.

Receive Salary & Remittances

Employers can pay your salary directly to your wallet. Also accepts international remittances from Western Union.

How It Works

Your Vodafone Cash wallet is directly tied to your Vodafone SIM card and is fully backed by Banque Misr — one of Egypt's largest state-owned banks — ensuring your money is safe and regulated. Every transaction requires your 4-digit PIN for authorization, adding a strong layer of security against unauthorized use. When you make or receive a payment, you instantly receive an SMS confirmation on your phone so you always know the status of your money. The system operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week through both the USSD menu (*9#) and the Vodafone Cash mobile app, giving you full control of your funds at any time.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1

Get a Vodafone SIM card

You need an active Vodafone Egypt number to use Vodafone Cash. Visit any Vodafone store with your National ID to obtain or activate a SIM card.

💡 Non-Vodafone users can also register for a Vodafone Cash wallet by visiting a Vodafone store — you don't need to switch your main number.

2

Register your wallet

Dial *9# on your phone and follow the on-screen prompts to create your wallet. You'll be asked to set a secure 4-digit PIN. For full activation, visit any Vodafone store with your National ID for biometric verification.

💡 Basic registration allows up to EGP 5,000 wallet limit. Completing full KYC verification at a store raises your limit to EGP 30,000.

3

Add money to your wallet

There are several ways to top up your Vodafone Cash balance:

  • • Visit any Vodafone agent or store and hand them cash
  • • Deposit at supported ATMs
  • • Transfer from your bank account via InstaPay
  • • Receive your salary or remittances directly

💡 Top-ups are processed instantly — your balance updates in real time as soon as the transaction is confirmed.

4

Pay bills and services

Dial *9# and select "Pay Bills," then choose the service category (electricity, water, gas, telecom, etc.). Enter your account or meter number and confirm the payment with your PIN.

You can also download the free Vodafone Cash app for a more comfortable, visual experience with additional features and an easier payment flow.

💡 Save recurring bills in the app for one-tap payments — no need to re-enter your account details every month.

5

Send money to others

Dial *9# and select "Send Money," then enter the recipient's Vodafone number, specify the amount, and confirm with your PIN. Transfers are instant and free between Vodafone Cash wallets. You can also send money to other banks via the InstaPay integration.

💡 Check the app or dial *9# to view current fees for transfers to bank accounts or other mobile wallets outside the Vodafone network.

Pro Tips

• Wallet limit goes up to EGP 30,000 for fully verified (KYC) accounts — complete verification at any Vodafone store to unlock the higher limit.
• Cash out at any Vodafone agent nationwide or at ATMs displaying the Vodafone Cash logo — no bank account required.
• Download the Vodafone Cash app for a better experience than the USSD (*9#) menu — it offers a clearer interface, transaction history, and faster bill payments.
• You can receive international remittances from Western Union directly into your Vodafone Cash wallet — no bank account needed to collect money sent from abroad.

Security Warning

Keep your PIN confidential at all times. Vodafone Cash agents and customer service representatives will never ask for your PIN under any circumstances. Report any suspicious activity or unauthorized transactions immediately by calling 888.
